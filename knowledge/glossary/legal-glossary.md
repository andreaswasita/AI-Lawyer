# Glosarium Hukum Indonesia — Indonesian Legal Glossary

> Glosarium dwibahasa Indonesia–Inggris untuk referensi cepat. Disusun berdasarkan kategori hukum.
> Bilingual Indonesian–English legal glossary for quick reference. Organized by legal category.

---

## Hukum Umum (General Law)

### Sumber Hukum & Peraturan (Sources of Law & Regulations)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Hukum** | Law | Seperangkat aturan yang mengatur perilaku masyarakat dan ditegakkan oleh negara. A set of rules governing society's conduct, enforced by the state. |
| 2 | **Undang-Undang (UU)** | Statute / Act | Peraturan yang dibuat oleh DPR bersama Presiden. Legislation enacted by Parliament together with the President. |
| 3 | **Peraturan Pemerintah (PP)** | Government Regulation | Peraturan yang ditetapkan Presiden untuk melaksanakan UU. Regulation issued by the President to implement a statute. |
| 4 | **Peraturan Daerah (Perda)** | Regional/Local Regulation | Peraturan yang ditetapkan oleh pemerintah daerah (provinsi/kabupaten/kota). Regulation enacted by regional government. |
| 5 | **Keputusan Presiden (Keppres)** | Presidential Decree | Keputusan Presiden yang bersifat penetapan (konkret, individual). A presidential decision of a concrete, individual nature. |
| 6 | **Peraturan Presiden (Perpres)** | Presidential Regulation | Peraturan yang ditetapkan Presiden untuk melaksanakan UU atau PP. Regulation issued by the President to implement statutes or government regulations. |
| 7 | **Peraturan Menteri (Permen)** | Ministerial Regulation | Peraturan yang dikeluarkan oleh menteri dalam lingkup kewenangannya. Regulation issued by a minister within their authority. |
| 8 | **Instruksi Presiden (Inpres)** | Presidential Instruction | Perintah Presiden kepada jajaran pemerintahan. Presidential directive to government apparatus. |
| 9 | **Surat Edaran (SE)** | Circular Letter | Pedoman internal yang tidak mengikat umum. Internal guideline without general binding force. |
| 10 | **Yurisprudensi** | Jurisprudence / Case Law | Putusan hakim terdahulu yang dijadikan pedoman. Prior court decisions used as precedent. |

### Dokumen & Surat Hukum (Legal Documents & Pleadings)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 11 | **Gugatan** | Lawsuit / Claim | Tuntutan hak yang diajukan ke pengadilan dalam perkara perdata. A civil claim filed before the court. |
| 12 | **Permohonan** | Petition / Application | Permintaan ke pengadilan yang bersifat voluntair (sepihak). A voluntary (ex parte) request to the court. |
| 13 | **Tuntutan** | Prosecution Demand / Requisitoir | Permintaan jaksa kepada hakim mengenai hukuman bagi terdakwa. The prosecutor's sentencing demand to the judge. |
| 14 | **Dakwaan** | Indictment / Charge | Surat yang memuat rumusan tindak pidana yang didakwakan. A document setting out criminal charges against the accused. |
| 15 | **Petitum** | Prayer for Relief | Bagian gugatan berisi tuntutan penggugat kepada pengadilan. The section of a lawsuit stating the relief sought. |
| 16 | **Posita** | Statement of Facts / Grounds | Bagian gugatan berisi uraian fakta dan dasar hukum. The section stating facts and legal basis of a claim. |
| 17 | **Eksepsi** | Objection / Preliminary Defense | Tangkisan tergugat di luar pokok perkara. Defendant's procedural objection outside the merits. |
| 18 | **Replik** | Reply | Tanggapan penggugat atas jawaban tergugat. Plaintiff's response to defendant's answer. |
| 19 | **Duplik** | Rejoinder | Tanggapan tergugat atas replik penggugat. Defendant's response to plaintiff's reply. |
| 20 | **Pledoi** | Defense Plea | Pembelaan terdakwa/penasehat hukum atas tuntutan jaksa. Defense counsel's plea in response to prosecution's demand. |
| 21 | **Somasi** | Formal Notice / Demand Letter | Surat peringatan resmi agar pihak lain memenuhi kewajibannya. A formal warning letter demanding performance of an obligation. |
| 22 | **Kesimpulan** | Closing Argument / Summary | Rangkuman akhir dari pihak berperkara sebelum putusan. Final summary submitted by parties before judgment. |
| 23 | **Memori Banding** | Appeal Brief | Dokumen alasan keberatan atas putusan untuk diajukan ke pengadilan tinggi. Written grounds of appeal to the High Court. |
| 24 | **Memori Kasasi** | Cassation Brief | Dokumen alasan keberatan atas putusan untuk diajukan ke Mahkamah Agung. Written grounds for cassation appeal to the Supreme Court. |

### Putusan & Upaya Hukum (Judgments & Legal Remedies)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 44 | **Putusan** | Judgment / Decision | Pernyataan hakim di sidang pengadilan yang bersifat menyelesaikan perkara. A court's decision resolving a case. |
| 45 | **Penetapan** | Court Order / Decree | Putusan pengadilan atas perkara permohonan (voluntair). Court decision on a petition (voluntary jurisdiction). |
| 46 | **Vonis** | Verdict / Sentence | Putusan hakim dalam perkara pidana mengenai hukuman. Judge's criminal verdict regarding sentencing. |
| 47 | **Inkracht (van gewijsde)** | Final and Binding | Putusan yang telah berkekuatan hukum tetap, tidak dapat dilawan lagi dengan upaya hukum biasa. A judgment that is legally final; no ordinary legal remedy remains. |
| 48 | **Kasasi** | Cassation | Upaya hukum ke Mahkamah Agung atas putusan pengadilan tinggi. Appeal to the Supreme Court against a High Court decision. |
| 49 | **Banding** | Appeal | Upaya hukum biasa ke pengadilan tinggi atas putusan pengadilan negeri. Ordinary appeal to the High Court against a District Court decision. |
| 50 | **Peninjauan Kembali (PK)** | Judicial Review / Extraordinary Appeal | Upaya hukum luar biasa atas putusan inkracht karena ditemukan novum. Extraordinary review of a final judgment due to new evidence. |
| 51 | **Grasi** | Clemency / Pardon | Pengampunan dari Presiden yang mengubah/menghapus pidana. Presidential pardon altering or eliminating a sentence. |
| 52 | **Amnesti** | Amnesty | Pengampunan dari Presiden yang menghapus tindak pidana tertentu, biasanya bersifat politis. Presidential amnesty abolishing certain offenses, usually political. |
| 53 | **Abolisi** | Abolition | Penghentian penuntutan pidana oleh Presiden sebelum putusan. Presidential order stopping prosecution before judgment. |
| 54 | **Rehabilitasi** | Rehabilitation | Pemulihan hak seseorang dalam kemampuan, kedudukan, harkat, dan martabatnya. Restoration of a person's rights, position, and dignity. |

### Konsep Hukum Perdata (Civil Law Concepts)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 55 | **Wanprestasi** | Breach of Contract / Default | Kelalaian atau kegagalan memenuhi kewajiban dalam perjanjian. Failure to perform contractual obligations. |
| 56 | **Perbuatan Melawan Hukum (PMH)** | Tort / Unlawful Act | Perbuatan yang melanggar hukum dan menimbulkan kerugian bagi pihak lain (Pasal 1365 KUHPerdata). Act violating law causing damage to another (Art. 1365 Civil Code). |
| 57 | **Overmacht / Force Majeure** | Force Majeure | Keadaan memaksa yang menghalangi pemenuhan kewajiban, di luar kendali pihak. Unforeseeable circumstances preventing performance, beyond a party's control. |
| 58 | **Materai** | Stamp Duty | Bea yang dikenakan atas dokumen tertentu agar sah sebagai alat bukti. Duty imposed on certain documents for evidentiary validity. |
| 59 | **Akta Otentik** | Authentic Deed | Akta yang dibuat oleh/di hadapan pejabat berwenang dengan kekuatan pembuktian sempurna. Deed made by/before an authorized official, with full evidentiary force. |
| 60 | **Akta di Bawah Tangan** | Private Deed / Unnotarized Deed | Akta yang dibuat tanpa perantara pejabat berwenang. Deed made without the involvement of an authorized official. |
| 61 | **Kuasa** | Power of Attorney | Surat pemberian wewenang kepada orang lain untuk bertindak atas nama pemberi kuasa. Document authorizing another to act on one's behalf. |
| 62 | **Domisili Hukum** | Legal Domicile | Alamat hukum yang dipilih para pihak untuk kepentingan perjanjian atau perkara. Chosen legal address for contractual or litigation purposes. |
| 63 | **Itikad Baik** | Good Faith | Kejujuran dan niat baik dalam melaksanakan perjanjian. Honesty and fair dealing in performing a contract. |
| 64 | **Perjanjian** | Agreement / Contract | Perbuatan hukum di mana satu pihak atau lebih mengikatkan diri (Pasal 1313 KUHPerdata). A legal act in which one or more parties bind themselves. |
| 65 | **Jaminan / Agunan** | Collateral / Security | Harta benda yang dijaminkan untuk pelunasan utang. Assets pledged to secure repayment of a debt. |
| 66 | **Cessie** | Assignment of Receivables | Pengalihan piutang atas nama dari kreditur lama ke kreditur baru. Transfer of a named receivable from old to new creditor. |

## Hukum Keluarga (Family Law)

### Perkawinan & Perceraian (Marriage & Divorce)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 67 | **Nikah / Perkawinan** | Marriage | Ikatan lahir batin antara seorang pria dan wanita sebagai suami istri (UU No. 1/1974). Legal bond between a man and woman as husband and wife. |
| 68 | **Cerai Gugat** | Divorce Petition (by wife) | Perceraian yang diajukan oleh istri di Pengadilan Agama. Divorce initiated by the wife at the Religious Court. |
| 69 | **Cerai Talak** | Divorce by Repudiation (by husband) | Perceraian yang diajukan oleh suami di Pengadilan Agama. Divorce initiated by the husband at the Religious Court. |
| 70 | **Talak** | Repudiation / Divorce Pronouncement | Ikrar suami di hadapan Pengadilan Agama untuk menceraikan istri. Husband's pronouncement of divorce before the Religious Court. |
| 71 | **Khuluk** | Khul' / Redemptive Divorce | Perceraian atas kehendak istri dengan membayar iwadh (tebusan). Divorce at wife's initiative with payment of compensation. |
| 72 | **Fasakh** | Annulment (Islamic) | Pembatalan perkawinan oleh Pengadilan Agama karena syarat nikah tidak terpenuhi. Annulment of marriage by Religious Court due to unfulfilled conditions. |
| 73 | **Iddah** | Waiting Period | Masa tunggu bagi istri setelah perceraian sebelum boleh menikah lagi. Waiting period for a wife after divorce before remarrying. |
| 74 | **Mut'ah** | Consolation Gift | Pemberian wajib dari suami kepada istri yang dicerai sebagai penghibur. Mandatory gift from husband to divorced wife as consolation. |
| 75 | **Mahar** | Dowry / Mahr | Pemberian wajib dari calon suami kepada calon istri saat nikah. Mandatory gift from groom to bride upon marriage. |
| 76 | **Wali Nikah** | Marriage Guardian | Orang yang berhak menikahkan mempelai perempuan. Person authorized to give away the bride in marriage. |
| 77 | **Taklik Talak** | Conditional Divorce Clause | Perjanjian yang digantungkan pada syarat tertentu; jika dilanggar, istri berhak mengajukan cerai. Conditional agreement; if violated, wife may file for divorce. |
| 78 | **Nusyuz** | Marital Disobedience | Pembangkangan istri terhadap kewajiban dalam rumah tangga menurut hukum Islam. Wife's disobedience to marital obligations under Islamic law. |
| 79 | **KDRT** | Domestic Violence | Kekerasan Dalam Rumah Tangga (UU No. 23/2004). Violence within the household. |
| 80 | **KUA** | Office of Religious Affairs | Kantor Urusan Agama; tempat pencatatan nikah bagi Muslim. Office for Muslim marriage registration. |
| 81 | **Pengadilan Agama (PA)** | Religious Court | Pengadilan yang menangani perkara hukum keluarga Islam. Court handling Islamic family law matters. |
| 82 | **Perjanjian Pranikah** | Prenuptial Agreement | Perjanjian tertulis sebelum perkawinan mengenai harta. Written pre-marriage agreement regarding assets. |
| 83 | **Nafkah** | Maintenance / Alimony | Kewajiban suami memberikan biaya hidup kepada istri dan anak. Husband's obligation to provide living expenses. |

|---|---|---|---|
| 84 | **Hadhanah** | Child Custody (Islamic) | Hak pemeliharaan anak setelah perceraian menurut hukum Islam. Right to child custody after divorce under Islamic law. |
| 85 | **Hak Asuh Anak** | Child Custody | Hak orang tua untuk merawat dan membesarkan anak setelah perceraian. Parental right to care for and raise children post-divorce. |

### Waris (Inheritance)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 89 | **Waris** | Inheritance / Succession | Peralihan harta dari pewaris kepada ahli waris. Transfer of estate from decedent to heirs. |

## Istilah Tambahan (Additional Terms)

| # | Istilah (Indonesian) | English | Definisi / Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 210 | **Hukum Acara** | Procedural Law | Hukum yang mengatur tata cara penegakan hukum materiil. Law governing procedures for enforcing substantive law. |
| 211 | **Hukum Materiil** | Substantive Law | Hukum yang mengatur hak dan kewajiban pihak-pihak. Law governing rights and obligations of parties. |
| 212 | **Asas Legalitas** | Principle of Legality | Tidak ada pidana tanpa undang-undang (nullum delictum, nulla poena sine lege). No crime, no punishment without law. |
