### The HukumOnline-Justika-Easybiz Vulnerability

Their ecosystem strength is also their weakness:
- **Fragmented experience**: 3 separate platforms for 3 different needs
- **No unified subscription**: Can't pay once for everything
- **B2B bias**: HukumOnline and Easybiz focus on enterprises, leaving individual consumers underserved
- **Legacy tech**: Rule-based templates, basic AI chatbot (AIlex is simple Q&A)
- **Price floor**: Justika's cheapest option (Rp 30k) still requires human involvement
